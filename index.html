What is Wheelie Awesome Socials?

Wheelie Awesome Socials is a Toronto community for wheelchair users. Most events also welcome rollator and scooter users. Events include:

  • Pub nights,
  • Museum visits,
  • Crafting workshops
  • Picnics
  • Local excursions
